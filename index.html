With NASA's OSIRIS-REx mission successfully returning samples from Bennu in 2023, the Psyche spacecraft studying a metal-rich asteroid (en route, arrival ~2029), and private ventures like AstroForge planning 2026 prospecting missions, the era of commercial space resource utilization is rapidly approaching — potentially worth quadrillions in untapped materials.
